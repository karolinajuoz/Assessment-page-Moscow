Moscow, Russia

About Moscow:

Moscow, the heart and soul of Russia, is a city of dazzling contrasts and rich heritage. The city hums with life, offering a mix of historical marvels, cultural treasures, and bustling modernity. You may wander through the sprawling "Gorky Park", absorb the magnificence of the Bolshoi Theatre, and explore the myriad of museums and galleries that showcase Russia's heritage.

My experience:

Last winter, I was fortunate enough to explore the beautiful city of Moscow. A highlight from my trip was ice-skating in "Gorky Park" under the twinkling lights. I made aquaintance with a local named Diana who later invited me to her family's dinner, where I enjoyed traditional Russian dishes like pelmeni and olivier salad and was welcomed with warm hospitality. Moscow's beautiful city and the kindness of its people left a lasting impression on me.
